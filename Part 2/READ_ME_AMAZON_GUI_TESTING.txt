1 - testSignInInvalidData
2 - testSignInWrongPassword
3 - testSignInValidData
4 - testSearchValidItem
5 - testSearchInvalidItem
6 - testAddItemToCart
7 - testCreateWishList
8 - testAddItemToWishList
